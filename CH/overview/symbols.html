National Symbols & Flag Meaning

Five-Star Red Flag

Symbolizes the great unity of the Chinese people under the leadership of the Communist Party of China (CPC). The red color represents revolution, and the five yellow stars stand for the CPC and the four social classes—workers, peasants, urban petty bourgeoisie, and national bourgeoisie.

National Emblem

Features Tiananmen Square, five stars, wheat ears, and a gear, embodying the worker-peasant alliance and the birth of New China under people’s democratic dictatorship.

National Anthem

"March of the Volunteers," rooted in the theme of resisting Japanese aggression and saving the nation.
