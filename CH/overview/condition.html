Basic National Conditions Summary

Conditions

China is a socialist country located in East Asia, on the western Pacific coast. It covers a land area of approximately 9.6 million square kilometers and has a coastline exceeding 18,000 kilometers. Land-boarding 14 countries and sharing maritime boundaries with 8 others, its administrative divisions include 4 municipalities, 23 provinces, 5 autonomous regions, and 2 SARs. China’s national development goal is to basically realize socialist modernization by 2035, with significant leaps in economic strength, science and technology, and overall national power.
