Climate Types & Seasonal Changes

Eastern Monsoon Region

Dominated by monsoons. Winters are cold and dry with northerly winds; summers are warm and humid with southerly winds. Subtypes include tropical monsoon, subtropical monsoon, and temperate monsoon climates in coastal areas.

Northwest Arid Region

Characterized by arid climates.

Qinghai-Tibet Plateau Alpine Region

Unique alpine climate.

Seasonal Changes

Spring: temperature rises. Summer: hot and rainy. Autumn: temperature drops, little rain. Winter: cold.
